Introduction
Welcome to the Automark app (hereinafter referred to as the "App"). This app is designed to showcase used cars for sale within the United Arab Emirates.
We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and adhere to data collection and storage practices that comply with applicable privacy regulations, including Google Play's Data Safety requirements.
What Data Do We Collect?
We collect the following categories of personal data:
Registration Information
When creating an account, we ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number.
Vehicle Information
When listing a car for sale, we ask you to provide vehicle details such as model, year, mileage, and price.
Location Information
We may collect location information to display cars for sale near you or to help you connect with nearby buyers.
How Do We Use Your Data?
Your data is used for the following purposes:
•	To provide the App's services, including displaying cars for sale and facilitating connections between buyers and sellers.
•	To improve your in-app experience, such as personalising content and displaying relevant advertisements.
•	To communicate important updates, service changes, or promotional offers.
•	To ensure the safety and security of our platform and users.
How Do We Protect Your Data?
We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:
•	Strong encryption and security protocols to protect data from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure.
•	Restricted access to personal data, limited to employees who require it to perform their job duties.
•	Regular reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices.
Data Sharing & Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers strictly for the purpose of delivering our services. These parties are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and secure.
Your Rights
As a user, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:
•	Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
•	Right to Rectification: You may ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
•	Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
•	Right to Object: You may object to certain types of data processing.
•	Right to Data Portability: You may request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
•	Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.
Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice within the App or by sending you an email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
