Changes to the Privacy Policy:
Registration Information: When creating an account on the app, we ask you to provide
some personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number.
Vehicle Information: When listing a car for sale on the app, we ask you to provide
information about the car such as its model, year, mileage, and price.
Location Information: We may collect location information for purposes such as
displaying cars for sale near you or helping you find a buyer for your car.

We use your data to provide the app's services, such as displaying cars for sale and
helping you find a buyer.
We use your data to improve your experience within the app, such as displaying ads that
are relevant to your interests.

We employ strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use,
or disclosure.
We restrict access to your data to employees who need it to perform their job duties.

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data.
You have the right to object to the processing of your data.
You have the right to data portability.
You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe that
your data has been processed unlawfully.

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes
by posting a notice on the app or sending you an email.
